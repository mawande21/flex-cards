Fire

Fire, also called combustion, is a chemical reaction which releases heat and fire. It requires two chemicals to react, on earth this is typically a fuel and oxygen.

Lighning

Lightning is a phenomenon which occurs when clouds, usually rainclouds, causes static electricity to build up. Lightning is the release of that electricity!

Mountain

Mountains occur when tectonic pressure pushes earth upwards. Geologists use them for all sorts of info.


Waterfall

Waterfalls occur when a river meets a cliff. Don't stand too close or you'll get wet!

Waves

Waves is a phenomenon which occurs in large bodies of water. It is most easily seen on beaches next to the ocean. They occur due to wind and currents, as well as other factors.
